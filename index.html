Hi! This little app can help you practice imagining music intervals. I decided to make it because I noticed that it's often easier for me to recognize a certain music structure, then play and hear it in my head. The app generated notes at random frequencies, so you don't get used to a particular tuning and can fully focus on grapsing the concept. The app will tell you which interval to imagine and whether the second note should be higher or lower. You can sing the second note, or and you will sing or imagine the second note in your head. If you prefer using numbers from 1-12 instead of the conventional names, you can switch to that.

Controls:
  • Space: new interval
  • Z: repeat the given note
  • X: play the answer
  • C: play both given note and the answer
  • Click to close this window

⬆︎Press Space to start⬇︎
